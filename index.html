F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How much does a website cost?

    We determine website prices based on a plethora of factors, mostly the complexity of the website. The starting rate is at Ksh 15,000/-.

  • How long does it take to have my website developed?

    The span of time it takes to develop a website depends on its complexity and the scope of work needed. For a normal website, delivery is within 28 days.

  • Do you offer warranty on repairs?

    We do not offer warranty on repairs. However, we offer after-repair servicing on devices.

  • What's the timeframe for repairing my phone/computer?

    This is highly dependent on the type of repair your device needs. Some repairs will take 30 minutes to a few hours so you get back your device on the same day. Others require logistics processes of importing required replacement parts which tend to take longer.

  • What is Phone software flashing?

    Phone flashing is when your phone software gets glitches failing to perform the required tasks, which means the Operating System must be reinstalled to resume normal operations. P.S To have your phone flashed, you need to provide proof of ownership of the device.

  • Do you provide offsite services?

    Yes we do. Some services require us to visit our clients premises, for example, maintenance of desktop computers in a company or setting up a Local Are Network for a school.
